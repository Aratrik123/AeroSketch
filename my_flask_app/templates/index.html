About Us

AeroSketch introduces a revolutionary way to create art and write in a digital manner, leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as OpenCV and MediaPipe. Using this you can unleash your creativity using nothing but your hands and gestures. Utilizing advanced computer vision algorithms, it detects intricate hand gestures in real-time, translating them into strokes and commands on a digital canvas. Whether you're sketching, writing notes, or painting, the experience is intuitive and responsive.

Getting started

Steps to follow to use AeroSketch

Click on 'Try AeroSketch v1.0' button.
Show your palm facing the webcam.
Select a colour by placing your index finger in the colour tabs.
Join the index finger and thumb to not draw.
Press 't' to toggle dark and light mode in the canvas.
Press 'c' to exit.
